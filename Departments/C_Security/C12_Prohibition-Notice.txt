[bold]This notice is to inform station personnel of a [color=#cb0000]prohibition[/color] on [color=#cb0000]consumption and possesion[/color] of [color=#cb0000]alcohol[/color], effective immediately. Those found in defiance of prohibition will be [color=#cb0000]prosecuted to the full extent of the law.[/color][/bold]

[color=#cb0000]   ▪  ▪  ▪[/color]

[head=1]Space Law Addendum:[/head]

[color=#cb0000][head=3]Consuption of Alcohol[/head][/color]        ▪ [bold]\[1-08\] - Minor[/bold]
   Those found with alcohol in their system may be charged with a [bold]\[1-08\][/bold], with repeat offenses potentially resulting in up to five (5) minutes in jail.

[color=#cb0000][head=3]Possesion of Alcohol[/head][/color]            ▪ [bold]\[2-08\] - Moderate[/bold]
   Those found in possesion of alcohol may be charged with a [bold]\[2-08\][/bold]. Alcohol will be confiscated, with up to five (5) minutes in jail for a first offense.

[color=#cb0000][head=3]Distribution of Alcohol[/head][/color]      ▪ [bold]\[3-08\] - Major[/bold]
   Those found to be distributing alcohol may be charged with a [bold]\[3-08\][/bold]. Alcohol will be confiscated, with up to ten (10) minutes in jail for a first offense.

[color=#cb0000]   ▪  ▪  ▪[/color]

[head=1]The Dangers of Alcohol:[/head]

   Heavy drinking can increase your risk of serious health problems, including: Certain cancers, such as colorectal cancer, breast cancer and cancers of the mouth, throat, esophagus and liver. Liver disease. Cardiovascular disease, such as high blood pressure and stroke.
